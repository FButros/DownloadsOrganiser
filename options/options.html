DownloadsOrganiser Options

Follow these steps to customize your download organization:

  1. Add a Folder: Click 'Add New Folder' and enter the file extension (e.g., 'pdf') and the desired folder name (e.g., 'PDFs/').
  2. Remove a Folder: Click 'Remove' next to the Folder you wish to delete.
  3. Save Your Settings: Click 'Save Changes' to apply your settings.

Your settings will automatically organize downloaded files into the specified folders.
